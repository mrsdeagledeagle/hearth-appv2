Welcome to Hearth

Hearth is your family's childcare command center. It keeps track of your nannies, your schedules, and your kids' care — and handles problems automatically while you sleep.

This quick tour takes about 60 seconds. Swipe or tap Next to continue.

😓

The problem

Childcare scheduling is stressful. Your nanny calls off at midnight. Daycare is randomly closed. One thing changes and everything cascades — who has the car, who can go to the office, who needs to stay home.

Hearth takes that weight off your plate — especially when you're asleep.

💤

Sleep mode

Turn on Sleep mode at bedtime. While you're sleeping, the agent watches everything and acts on your behalf — no waking you up.

Example: Your nanny texts at 11 PM that she can't come Friday. Hearth texts your backup nannies, gets a confirmation, updates the schedule, and sends you a summary at 7 AM. You wake up to a solved problem.
◈

Dashboard

Your first stop every morning. It shows:

  • Alert cards — things the agent found or resolved overnight
  • Today's coverage — who's watching the kids and when
  • Logistics — who has the car, who's WFH vs in office
  • Stats — open gaps, upcoming flags, agent actions

Tap the alert buttons to approve agent plans or dismiss resolved items.

📅

Calendar

One view combining every schedule that affects childcare — color-coded so you can instantly see what's happening.

Nanny roster

Add your primary nanny and as many backups as you want — with their phone number, hourly rate, and priority order.

Nannies never need to download anything. The agent contacts them by SMS only. Whoever replies YES first gets the shift.

You can also view the full SMS thread here — every message the agent sent and received, with timestamps.
🔭

Lookahead & pay log

30-day Lookahead — the agent scans ahead every night and flags upcoming problems: daycare closed, school trips, a parent offsite. You can tap "Agent handle" to let it solve each one automatically.

Hours & pay log — every Saturday morning the agent tallies who worked what hours and at what rate. Use it to pay your nannies — Hearth never handles money.

🌿

You're all set

Right now you're looking at demo data — simulated schedules so you can explore safely before connecting real calendars.

When you're ready to go live, head to Settings and connect your Google Calendars one by one. The app works the same way — just with your real schedules.

You can replay this tour anytime from Settings → "Replay tour".
